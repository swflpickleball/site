Our Approach: Consistent Steps

Training a little every day in pickleball is essential to achieving success and improving in the sport. Thats what we are all about at PP&T. By consistently working on fundamental skills such as footwork, hand-eye coordination, and shot placement, you will be amazed at your own capabilities! PP&T is by your side as you go from zero to hero. Our academy utilizes paid packages, that way we can commit to seeing our students improve. Together we set goals, and cater your individualized program towards your own aspirations.
